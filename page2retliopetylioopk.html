What you need to know about couple therapy

When your couple runs through a difficult period, that it is in crisis and that the best advice of your friends (or the Internet) seem to not succeed in reversing the trend, the break can often appear as a fatality.But there is an alternative: that of collecting the help and advice of a person (psychologist, couple therapist) whose specialty is precisely to help couples find solutions to come out of their conflict situation.In this article, we make a focus on torque therapy.

Everyone knows about what is an individual psychological consultation, well a torque therapy is about the same principle but two.While individual therapy will focus on you, your psychology and well-being, the couple therapy will focus on your couple and its future, and the individual well-being that each of the partners will derive it will be the effect of the effects ofThe therapy on the couple.

The couple therapy is not intended to focus on one of the partners, nor for the role of being a referee, a judge or lawyer able to distribute points or defend a person.Couple therapy will remain focused on the couple, not on the interests of any of the partners.The goal is to find tracks, objectives, exercises and solutions to allow the couple to take a new direction, and thus to the partners to feel better.
